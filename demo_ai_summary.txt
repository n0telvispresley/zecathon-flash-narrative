# Strategic PR Impact Assessment & Performance Audit
**Zenith Bank Plc**  
**Period:** October 2024 – March 2025

---

### 1. Executive Summary
This report audits Zenith Bank Plc's media and reputation from October 2024 to March 2025.

Overall, Zenith maintained a **dominant and highly positive (92.1%)** brand reputation, driven by **Corporate Performance** (N350.4Bn Hybrid Capital Raise [cite: 563]) and **CEO Dr. Adaora Umeoji** prominence [cite: 562].

Competitively, Zenith held **28% Share of Voice (SOV)**, second place. It trails Access Bank (35%) but outperforms GTCO (20%) and First Bank (17%).

Key vulnerability: **Product/Service** sentiment. Low volume (4% of mentions), but **mobile app downtime** and **transaction failures** drive over 90% of **'Anger'** sentiment.

---

### 2. SWOT Analysis
* **Strengths**
  * **Strong Financials & Corporate News:** 92.1% positive sentiment from capital raise and results [cite: 563].
  * **CEO Prominence:** Dr. Adaora Umeoji ranks #1 in CEO media [cite: 562].
  * **Balanced Thematic Coverage:** Corporate (40%) and CSR/ESG (25%).

* **Weaknesses**
  * **Negative Product Sentiment:** 'Anger' tied to app performance.
  * **Low Message Penetration:** "Enhancing E-Channel Services" MPI at 4.5%.
  * **Legal/Risk Exposure:** EFCC court incident needs monitoring [cite: 563].

* **Opportunities**
  * **Digital Banking Narrative:** Use CBN PoS geo-tagging rules [cite: 564] to reposition E-Channel campaign.
  * **Consolidate SOV Lead:** Capture First Bank's weakened share.

* **Threats**
  * **Competitor Sponsorship:** Access Bank gains SOV via Lagos Marathon [cite: 563].
  * **Industry-Wide Negativity:** CBN fines and service issues create halo risk [cite: 549].

### 13. Principle & Methodology
Generated by **Flash Narrative AI Platform**. Analyzed **1,420 mentions** (Oct 2024–Mar 2025) from 50+ Nigerian outlets, 20 blogs, r/Nigeria. Demo uses static dataset.

---

### Recommendations
* **Amplify CEO & Corporate Wins:** Double promotion of N350.4Bn raise and Dr. Umeoji’s leadership to reinforce stability.
* **Fix App Perception Fast:** Launch transparent PR campaign acknowledging downtime, sharing fixes, and timeline.
* **Reboot E-Channel Messaging:** Tie campaign to CBN PoS geo-tagging rules – position Zenith as digital security leader.
* **Exploit First Bank Weakness:** Subtle “Trust & Governance” push contrasting Zenith’s clean record.
* **Boost International Exposure:** Target 1–2 high-impact global finance outlets per quarter.
